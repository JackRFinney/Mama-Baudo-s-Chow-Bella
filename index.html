Mama Baudo's Chow Bella is the newest restaurant from the Baudo's family.
With a rich heritage of well over 50 years, the Italian recipes handed down through generations feature rich
sauces, original pasta techniques, and home-made desserts. Delicious food!
Located at 429 Walker Road in Jackson, Tennessee (in Thomsen Farms), the restaurant features casual seating,
on-line drive-through ordering, and eco-friendly beverages and packaging. Alcohol is not served, but BYOB
wine is welcome with corkage fee.

You can also find us on WAITR and GrubHub!
